ABOUT US

GREENTEEN is family

Volunteer organization of young and active people who care about the future of our city. We unite teenagers and young people to make the city cleaner, cozier and greener together. We gather to clean streets, parks and public spaces and eliminate litter. Our actions not only help to improve the environment, but also inspire others to join the movement for a clean and green city.
